HR Policies and Procedures

1. Annual Leave Policy
- Full-time employees receive 20 days of annual leave per year
- Part-time employees receive pro-rated leave based on hours worked
- Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance for planned vacations
- Unused leave can be carried over up to 5 days to the next year
- Leave requests require manager approval

2. Sick Leave Policy
- Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year
- Medical certificate required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days
- Sick leave does not carry over to the next year
- Notify your manager as soon as possible if taking sick leave

3. Remote Work Policy
- Eligible after 3 months of employment
- Up to 3 days per week remote work allowed with manager approval
- Must be available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM)
- Maintain secure home office setup
- Company equipment must be used for work

4. Performance Reviews
- Annual performance reviews conducted in December
- Mid-year check-ins scheduled in June
- Reviews based on goals, competencies, and values alignment
- Self-assessment required before review meeting

5. Professional Development
- $2,000 annual budget per employee for training and conferences
- Must be relevant to current role or career development plan
- Submit training request form 30 days in advance
- Share learnings with team after attending training

6. Benefits Enrollment
- Open enrollment period: November 1-30 annually
- New hires have 30 days from start date to enroll
- Life events allow mid-year changes
- Contact HR for questions about coverage

7. Workplace Conduct
- Respectful and professional behavior expected at all times
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination
- Report concerns to HR or use anonymous hotline
- Retaliation against reporters is prohibited

8. Onboarding Process
- Week 1: Orientation, IT setup, meet team
- Week 2-4: Role-specific training and shadowing
- 30-60-90 day check-ins with manager
- Buddy assigned for first 3 months

9. Time Tracking
- All employees must log hours in the time tracking system
- Submit timesheet by 5 PM each Friday
- Overtime requires prior manager approval
- Accurate time recording is mandatory

10. Exit Process
- Provide 2 weeks notice for resignation
- Exit interview conducted by HR
- Return all company property
- Final paycheck includes unused vacation payout
- Benefits continue through end of month
